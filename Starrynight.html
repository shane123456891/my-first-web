Starry Night

Starry Night is an oil painting by Dutch artist Vincent van Gogh, created in 1889. It is one of his most famous works and is often considered a symbol of the artist's emotional depth and vivid imagination. The painting depicts a night sky swirling with stars over a small town, set against a peaceful mountainous landscape. Starry Night is housed at the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City.
